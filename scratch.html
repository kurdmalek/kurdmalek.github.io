Autonomous Freight Aircraft | Natilus Inc.

Natilus is seeking to disrupt the freight forwarding market by introducing large, unmanned aircraft characterized by significantly reduced operating costs. I developed aircraft simulation models for FEA in aerodynamics and hydrodynamics. I also worked in generating computer-aided models for scaled composite manufacturing.

NASA 2025 Supersonic Transport | Goldeneye AB1 (NASA Competition), (UC Berkeley press release)

We designed a commercial supersonic business aircraft to meet NASA performance and environmental goals by 2025. As a submission to the 2016-17 NASA University Aeronautics Design Challenge, Goldeneye AB1 featrues a high-lift, variable-surface geometry wing enabling efficient subsonic and supersonic cruise. Our team was invited to present our work at the NASA Langley University Aeronautics Symposium, where we claimed Honors.
