Engineering

Autonomous Freight Aircraft | Natilus Inc.
Natilus is seeking to disrupt the freight forwarding market by introducing large, unmanned aircraft characterized by significantly reduced operating costs. I developed aircraft simulation models for FEA in aerodynamics and hydrodynamics. I also worked in generating computer-aided models for scaled composite manufacturing.

NASA 2025 Supersonic Transport | Goldeneye AB1
Designed a commercial supersonic business aircraft to meet NASA performance and environmental goals by 2025. As a submission to the 2016-17 NASA University Aeronautics Design Challenge, Goldeneye AB1 featrues a high-lift, variable-surface geometry wing enabling efficient subsonic and supersonic cruise. Our team was invited to present our work at the NASA Langley University Aeronautics Symposium, where we claimed Honors.

Research | Spherical Tensegrity Robots

  • TT-Unisphere
    Tensegrity robot featuring actuator-enabled lattice, developed in collaboration with Adrian Agogino at NASA Ames Research Center. Offeres a simplified solution from preceding tensegrity robots based off of cable-governed actuation schemes. Fully modular assembly, delivering radical improvements in deployment and transitioning between passive and actuated states. Wireless functionality permitting command of several robot slaves under a single control policy.

  • TT-4mini V2 Spherical Tensegrity Robot
    First planetary scouting vehicle capable of climbing inclines exceeding 24 degrees, featuring multi-cable actuation control scheme. Developed tensegrity structure and conducted hardware validation experiments.

  • TT-4mini V1 Spherical Tensegrity Robot
    Rapid prototyping platform for tensegrity robots that reduces manufacture and assembly time.
